### Millets

- Foxtail Millet (Kangni (कंगनी) or Kakum in Hindi)
- Kodo millet is called कोदो (Kodo) or कोडोधन (Kododhana) in Hindi
- Barnyard millet is most commonly known as Sanwa (सनवा), Samak (सामक), or Jhangora (झंगोरा) in Hindi.
- Brown top millet is called हरी कंगनी (Hari Kangni) or छोटी कंगनी (Chhoti Kangni) in Hindi
- Little millet is known as Kutki or Sama in Hindi.
